Kohort Formation

In Kohortz, personality and lifestyle balance are just as big of factors as a CV. As users define their team preferences and set up “shop”. They decide the size, scope and culture of their freelance team. Once a team is formed, it’s able to link up with other teams, establish preferred partnerships and provide services larger than those they would be able to provide individually – without having to outbid (underbid) their freelancing counterparts.

Kohort Culture

Teams define the type of work they want to do, when they want to work and the type of clients they want to take on. Established teams can set up a storefront to show off past work and services to entice clients – all the while each member of the team continues to operate as an independent contractor, filing taxes and managing personal operations in their own country and in the same way they always have. As teams grow apart or see the need to provide a more diverse selection of services, they can disband, reorganize or merge with another team - feeling confident that the Kohortz platform will match them with those that share their culture, personality and sense of balance.

Kohort Tools

With add-ons and integrations with existing project management, time tracking, financial managements, gamification, decision making and advertising applications, Kohortz doesn’t try to reinvent the wheel when it comes to helping teams function and thrive. If an application exists that can help teams perform better, Kohortz will find a way integrate and provide the application on our platform.
