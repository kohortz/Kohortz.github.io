Kohortz for Clients

Find your new favorite freelance team.

Clients love Kohortz as well, because they are able to hire based on projects, timelines and strategy, without the headache of wrangling and managing the freelancers themselves. Instead of wasting time searching through pages and pages of freelancers or companies only to be burned by a lack of responsiveness or poor deliverables, Kohortz will match a client’s request with a manageable list of teams that meet the client's criteria. Want to pay less, get more “interesting” work and go out on a limb – changes your request criteria to match your propensity for risk? We have tons of young and hungry teams ready to prove themselves. Want an established team with a proven track record, consistently on time deliverables and always incredible work? You may have to pay for it and wait in line, but we have the data to back up the fact that they won’t let you down. We can provide this to prospective clients because Kohortz has a robust reputation system and portfolio manager. Clients are able to see examples of the type of work they will be getting and have an estimate of how long similar work should take to complete. Over time, a client may even find that they are able to downsize their on-site work force in favor of a smattering of project based Kohortz teams. But don’t get too freewheeling… Clients gain reputations as well – providing interesting work and paying on time will ensure that you are never at a loss of teams jump on your jobs. Stroking an ego, being an all-around jerk or delaying payment on a job well done and you might get run out of town.
